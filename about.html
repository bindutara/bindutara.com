Bindutara Enterprises is a Kolkata-based transport solution company focused on empowering individuals by enabling car ownership through flexible rent-to-own schemes. We aim to support unemployed youth, self-employed drivers, and aspiring fleet operators by providing them access to brand new cars such as WagonR, Swift, Ertiga, and Tigor EVs, without the burden of upfront costs.

Our mission is to revolutionize the commercial vehicle space with eco-conscious choices, including electric vehicle (EV) conversions and sustainable operations. We partner with cab aggregators and offer flexible repayment models. We also help our drivers with government-backed financial schemes and vehicle loans through recognized programs such as Startup India and Pragati.
