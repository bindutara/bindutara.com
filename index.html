What We Do

We provide commercial vehicle leasing and rent-to-own solutions for gig economy drivers and small fleet operators, enabling them to access vehicles without heavy upfront costs.

  • By offering daily/weekly/monthly flexible plans tailored to drivers' earning cycles

Because we believe in empowering livelihoods and redefining mobility. Our mission is to help gig workers and driver-partners become vehicle owners, increase their earnings, and improve financial inclusion—while creating a scalable, tech-enabled fleet ecosystem in India.

1. Nature of the Agreement

bindutara.com offers vehicles under a structured long-term rent-to-own plan typically lasting 60 months (5 years) divided into multiple 11 months contracts. Renewal is not automatic — must be confirmed by a signed "Renewal Addendum" for each term.

Under this plan, the customer pays a recurring rental/lease, which may be daily, weekly, or monthly, as defined in the customer's signed agreement. These payments contribute toward eventual ownership of the vehicle.

The vehicle remains the legal property of bindutara.com until the full term is completed and all dues, including any outstanding amounts or penalties, are paid.

2. Contract Term and Ownership Transfer

The standard rental term is 60 months, unless otherwise stated in the specific contract signed between the customer and the company.

Upon completion of all scheduled payments and any applicable charges (including foreclosure, penalties, or legal fees), ownership of the vehicle will be transferred to the customer.

The responsibility and cost of name transfer in the registration and associated government records shall lie solely with the customer.

3. Payment Terms

Payment options shall be defined in the agreement and may be: Daily, Weekly, or Monthly.

The mode and schedule of payments will be selected at the start of the contract and cannot be altered without written approval from the company.

Failure to pay on time may result in late fees, service interruptions, vehicle seizure, or contract termination.

4. Commercial Use and Liability

Vehicles are intended for use in commercial passenger transport services, including but not limited to: Ola, Uber, Yatri Sathi, InDrive, Rapido, Red Taxi, Suttle.

Customers must comply with all state and central transport regulations, including passenger safety and commercial driving license requirements.

The company is not liable for customer misuse, passenger disputes, traffic violations, or negligence.

5. Customer Obligations

Customers are expected to:

  • Keep the vehicle in good working condition
  • Conduct routine maintenance
  • Avoid misuse or unauthorized modifications
  • Not sublease the vehicle

All costs for wear-and-tear, maintenance, and operational fuel are borne by the customer unless otherwise stated in the specific contract.

6. Insurance Requirements

The customer shall be responsible for ensuring that the third-party insurance for the vehicle is renewed before its expiry in compliance with applicable motor vehicle laws.

Failure to renew insurance may result in legal penalties and contract suspension.

Health Insurance: The lessee must maintain an active personal health insurance policy with a minimum coverage of ₹2,00,000 throughout the duration of the lease.

Life Insurance: The Lessee shall maintain a valid term life insurance policy with a minimum coverage of ₹5,00,000 for the entire duration of this Agreement.

7. Security Deposit

The Lessee shall pay the Company a total security deposit of ₹20,000 (Rupees Twenty Thousand only) as a refundable, non-interest-bearing deposit.

Upon successful completion of lock-in period and full agreement tenure, after deduction of any amounts due towards damages, penalties, outstanding payments, or other contractual obligations, the Company shall refund the security deposit within thirty (30) working days.

The security deposit shall not, under any circumstances, be adjusted against rental payments during the term of this Agreement.

8. Early Surrender and Cashback

If the Customer voluntarily surrenders the contract after completing a minimum of 730 fully paid days without default, and within a total of 835 days from the start date, the Company shall pay a cashback of ₹200 per paid day.

This cashback is applicable only in the event of early surrender after 730 full paid days. If the Customer surrenders before completing 730 full paid days, no cashback shall be provided.

If the Customer surrenders the contract at any time, no future claim to ownership of the vehicle shall be entertained.

9. Breach and Termination

The Company may immediately terminate the agreement without notice if:

  • Any clause in these Terms is breached
  • Fraud is detected
  • Payments are defaulted
  • Illegal use is identified

If the customer cancels the contract, a cancellation penalty will apply, all balance rent and fees must be paid, and the vehicle returned in acceptable condition.

10. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes shall be attempted to be resolved amicably, followed by arbitration under the Indian Arbitration and Conciliation Act. Jurisdiction shall be in Kolkata, West Bengal, India.

Refund and Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: August 21, 2025

Important Notice: Please read this policy carefully before signing any agreement or making payments.

No Cancellation Post Signup

Once the customer has signed the agreement and payment has been made for onboarding, processing, or initial rental, no cancellation will be entertained under any circumstances. This applies regardless of whether the vehicle has been assigned, delivered, or used.

No Refund After Contract Execution

After the rent-to-own contract is signed and activated, all payments made (security deposit, processing fees, daily/monthly rental, etc.) are non-refundable. Customers are expected to complete the full contract term as agreed unless otherwise stated in the specific contract.

Refund Eligibility (Limited Exceptions)

Refunds will only be considered in rare, valid exceptions including:

  • Vehicle unavailability from the company's end before onboarding
  • Duplicate payment errors reported within 24 hours of the transaction
  • Advance payments made by mistake before agreement signing (subject to case review)

Refund Processing Timeline

In case of a valid refund eligibility, the refund process may take a minimum of 7 working days and up to 30 working days from the date of confirmation by our customer support team. The refund will be credited to the original payment mode only.

Contract Completion

At the end of the contract term (or full repayment as per agreement), no separate refund is applicable. The customer is entitled to ownership transfer of the vehicle after all dues are cleared, but any rent paid throughout the term remains non-refundable.

Early Termination or Foreclosure

If the customer initiates early contract termination or foreclosure, all payments made till date (rent, deposit, processing, interest) shall be treated as consumed. No refund shall be processed. Foreclosure must be done as per clause mentioned in your agreement, with all outstanding dues settled.

Security Deposit Adjustments

If a security deposit is collected and contract is completed successfully, the deposit may be adjusted toward name transfer or final dues. If the vehicle is returned with damage or dues pending, deductions will apply. No interest is paid on deposits.

Dispute and Final Authority

Any refund or exception is subject to management's internal review and discretion. bindutara.com reserves the right to decline refund requests if found inconsistent with policy terms. All disputes shall be resolved under Kolkata jurisdiction.
