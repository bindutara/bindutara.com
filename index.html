1. Nature of the Agreement

Bindutara Enterprises offers vehicles under a structured long-term rent-to-own plan typically lasting up to 60 months (5 years) divided into multiple 11-month contracts. Renewal is not automatic — it must be confirmed by a signed "Renewal Addendum" for each term.

Under this plan, the customer pays a recurring rental/lease, which may be daily, weekly, or monthly, as defined in the customer’s signed agreement. These payments contribute toward eventual ownership of the vehicle.

The vehicle remains the legal property of Bindutara Enterprises until the full term is completed and all dues, including any outstanding amounts or penalties, are paid.

2. Contract Term and Ownership Transfer

The standard rental term is 36 to 60 months unless otherwise stated in the specific contract signed between the customer and the company.

Upon completion of all scheduled payments and applicable charges, ownership of the vehicle will be transferred to the customer. The responsibility and cost of name transfer in registration shall lie solely with the customer.

If the contract ends early due to foreclosure or pre-closure, the customer must ensure all outstanding payments, penalties, and charges are settled before title transfer.

3. Payment Terms

Payment options (Advace only) may be Daily, Weekly, or Monthly, as defined in the agreement. The mode and schedule of payments will be selected at the start of the contract and cannot be altered without written approval from the company.

Failure to pay on time may result in late fees, service interruptions, vehicle seizure, or contract termination.

4. Commercial Use and Liability

Vehicles are intended for commercial passenger transport services such as Ola, Uber, Rapido, Yatri Sathi, Red Taxi, etc.

Customers must comply with all transport regulations. The company is not liable for misuse, passenger disputes, traffic violations, or negligence.

5. Customer Obligations

Customers must keep the vehicle in good working condition, conduct maintenance, avoid misuse or unauthorized modifications, and not sublease the vehicle.

All costs for fuel, wear-and-tear, and maintenance are borne by the customer unless otherwise stated in the agreement.

6. Insurance

The customer is responsible for ensuring third-party insurance is renewed before expiry in compliance with law. Failure to renew may result in contract suspension.

Additionally, customers must maintain active health insurance (₹2,00,000 coverage minimum) and term life insurance (₹5,00,000 coverage minimum) during the agreement period.

7. Inspection and Monitoring

The company reserves the right to inspect the vehicle at any reasonable time. Vehicles may have GPS/IoT monitoring. Tampering may result in contract termination and legal action.

8. Breach and Termination

The company may terminate the agreement without notice if any clause is breached, fraud is detected, payments defaulted, or illegal use is identified.

If the customer cancels, penalties will apply and the vehicle must be returned in good condition. There is also a notice period of 30 day and a lock in period of 6 months

9. Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure

All shared business, financial, and personal information is confidential. Neither party shall disclose without written consent. This clause survives termination.

10. Non-Compete Clause

Customers may not engage in a competing vehicle rental or rent-to-own business during the tenure and for 2 years after termination. Violation may lead to penalties or legal action.

11. Communication Disclaimer

Refund and Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: August 21, 2025

Important Notice: Please read this policy carefully before signing any agreement or making payments.

No Cancellation Post Signup

Once the customer has signed the agreement and payment has been made for onboarding, processing, or initial rental, no cancellation will be entertained under any circumstances. This applies regardless of whether the vehicle has been assigned, delivered, or used.

No Refund After Contract Execution

After the rent-to-own contract is signed and activated, all payments made (security deposit, processing fees, daily/monthly rental, etc.) are non-refundable. Customers are expected to complete the full contract term as agreed unless otherwise stated in the specific contract.

Refund Eligibility (Limited Exceptions)

Refunds will only be considered in rare, valid exceptions including:

  • Vehicle unavailability from the company's end before onboarding
  • Duplicate payment errors reported within 24 hours of the transaction
  • Advance payments made by mistake before agreement signing (subject to case review)

Refund Processing Timeline

In case of a valid refund eligibility, the refund process may take a minimum of 7 working days and up to 30 working days from the date of confirmation by our customer support team. The refund will be credited to the original payment mode only.

Contract Completion

At the end of the contract term (or full repayment as per agreement), no separate refund is applicable. The customer is entitled to ownership transfer of the vehicle after all dues are cleared, but any rent paid throughout the term remains non-refundable.

Early Termination or Foreclosure

If the customer initiates early contract termination or foreclosure, all payments made till date (rent, deposit, processing, interest) shall be treated as consumed. No refund shall be processed. Foreclosure must be done as per clause mentioned in your agreement, with all outstanding dues settled.

Security Deposit Adjustments

If a security deposit is collected and contract is completed successfully, the deposit may be adjusted toward name transfer or final dues. If the vehicle is returned with damage or dues pending, deductions will apply. No interest is paid on deposits.

Dispute and Final Authority

Any refund or exception is subject to management's internal review and discretion. bindutara.com reserves the right to decline refund requests if found inconsistent with policy terms. All disputes shall be resolved under Kolkata jurisdiction.
