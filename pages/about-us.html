About Us

Founded in May 2025, Nytrix Bindutara Enterprises Pvt Ltd is a cutting-edge transport startup dedicated to empowering India's gig economy through an innovative rent-to-own vehicle model. Based in Kolkata, West Bengal, we aim to make vehicle ownership accessible for gig workers such as drivers on platforms like Ola, Uber, Rapido, and InDrive, while offering flexible and commercially viable leasing terms.

Our mission is to build a bridge between aspiration and accessibility. Whether you're a first-time entrepreneur, an intercity taxi driver, or a delivery agent, we provide long-term leasing (up to 60 months) with transparent terms, affordable daily or monthly payments, and end-of-contract vehicle ownership.

Nytrix Bindutara has proudly achieved key national recognitions that validate our business model and compliance standards:

  • DPIIT Startup India Recognition: Officially recognized under the Startup India initiative by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT).
  • Udyam/MSME Registration: Certified as a Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise (MSME), enabling access to financial and legal benefits.
  • Trade License: Fully licensed and registered with the local municipal authority in Kolkata, West Bengal.
  • Active Business Bank Account: Legally compliant banking structure with transaction-ready financial systems.
  • ROC and Tax Compliance: All necessary filings with the Registrar of Companies (ROC) and statutory departments are up-to-date and maintained.

Our foundational setup is strong. We have invested in securing our brand assets including the official website, company logo, digital presence, and a mobile APK for internal fleet management.

With a projected fleet size of 20000+ vehicles, Bindutara is actively engaging in fundraising and grant acquisition to scale operations, onboard drivers, and expand across metropolitan regions in India. Our business model is structured to serve B2B clients and self-employed individuals alike, offering reliable income-generating opportunities through transport assets.
